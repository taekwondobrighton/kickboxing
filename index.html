Kickboxing Brighton classes each week for adults & teens. On Tuesday & Thursday evenings. These kickboxing classes are designed for all levels, people of all abilities and we welcome beginners. High intensity cardio work outs with body weight exercises, designed to burn fat and tone muscle , Brighton Kickboxing fitness will help you get fighting fit. The culture of the teaching is designed to accommodate your personal goals.

Our training program will help you build a lean, toned physique while having a lot of fun and learning some basic self-defence. Based around Taekwon-Do punching, striking, blocking and kicking techniques we provided a full body workout learning the basics of kickboxing. These are dynamic and fun classes.

CARDIO

Cardio Training at Brighton Kickboxing. Combining martial arts techniques and cardio, kickboxing is a high-energy workout that is guaranteed to burn calories and fat.

TONING

There’s a large toning component to kickboxing because it forces you to engage all the muscle groups in your body.

CORE

By using kickboxing pads, as opposed to simply air boxing, you’ll burn even more calories. Due to the nature of kickboxing techniques, employing the core muscles, you will work your back and trim your waist.
